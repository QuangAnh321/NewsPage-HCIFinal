Here's how to play your Steam library on your iPhone or iPad

After a delay that lasted a full year, Valve recently released the iOS version of its Steam Link app in the App Store. The app was originally set for release in early 2018, but Apple ultimately rejected the app, postponing its launch. Steam Link ($52 at Amazon) allows you to play games in your Steam library on your iPhone ($1,000 at Amazon) or iPad ($249 at Walmart) as if you were gaming on your computer.
